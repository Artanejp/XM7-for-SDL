** F-Font For XM7/SDL **

Author:モ〜リン　<KFD00403@nifty.com>
Apply for libagar: K.Ohta <whaitsthis.sowhat@gmail.com>

Description:
　"F-Font" is emulated FM-7's tty fonts on subsystem.
  Made by モ〜リン who developed by N-Font,emulated PC8001,
order from Laver <mb25010@gmail.com>.
  K.Ohta applied  F_Font_Symbol.ttf to libagar's font system,
change encode LATIN-SYMBOL to ISO10646, F-Font_Symbol*_Unicode.ttf.

Usage:
 Place below fonts ~/.xm7 or /usr/local/share/xm7 ：
 F-Font_400line.ttf
 F-Font_Symbol_Unicode.ttf
