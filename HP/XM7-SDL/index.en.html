Install:

- make ; sudo make install　(0.2β or later)。
But not bundled libagar's sourcecode and fonts.
- You need AGAR1.4.1, OpenGL,SDL and SDL_Mixer on playing.
(I bundled binary of libagar on xm7-binary-package...) - Strong recommand 1.4.1SVN9049 or later .(Sourcecode of r9049)
- You need more font:IPA Gothic(V3). If not include your system, place to $HOME or current directory.(NEW!)
- Install bin/xm7 and bin/xm7.debug to /usr/local/bin.
- Install items below src/resource/ to /usr/local/share/xm7 .
- Install libagar/ to /usr/local . You must it , from dependency of libagar.
- you must "sudo ldconfig".
